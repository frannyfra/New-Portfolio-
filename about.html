About Francesca

Inquisitive, striving and resilient person. Following my experience at Founders and Coders, where we built weekly projects in teams, I have kicked off my career in software development and had the opportunity to work on some personal projects, strengthening my logical mindset and my ability to break down large problems into smaller chunks. My background in customer service has taught me the importance of empathy and attention to detail, which I’ve combined with my coding skills in order to deliver accessible and user-centered apps.


I’m looking forward to joining a team where I can collaborate with my skills and experience, and am excited to learn more in an environment with senior developers.
